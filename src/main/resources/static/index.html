Welcome to our Milwaukee wedding! We could not be more excited to have you be a part of our celebration! 

We met when we were just 16 years old as fate found us both working a summer job as lifeguards in suburban Chicago. Fast forward 11 years later and here we are getting married! The years in between created lots of logistical challenges as our college years were spent in different locations: Allison at University of Michigan and Mark at Michigan State. Thankfully our love was stronger than our school rivalry and we made it work from long distance through undergrad and later when Allison was in grad school in Cleveland and Mark landed his first job in Milwaukee.  Following grad school, Allison joined Mark in Wisconsin, and we are loving life in Milwaukee.

  We hope you’ll enjoy our adopted city as much as we do . . . check out our beautiful Lake Michigan lakefront, dozens of world class breweries, explore the historic third ward and don’t forget to try our famous cheese curds!
